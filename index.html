Daily Harvest Marketing Plan

Americans are not consuming enough fruits and vegetables as they should be.

  • Guidelines advise that adults should consume 1.5–2 cup-equivalents of fruits and 2–3 cup-equivalents of vegetables daily.
  • A higher proportion of women meet both fruit and vegetable recommendations than men.
  • About 90% eat too few vegetables, and 80% too few fruit.

Rising Demand for Convenient, Healthy Options

The increasing consumer demand for healthy, convenient, and nutrient-rich beverages, alongside the rising popularity of plant-based and functional food trends, is contributing to the growth of the global smoothies market.
